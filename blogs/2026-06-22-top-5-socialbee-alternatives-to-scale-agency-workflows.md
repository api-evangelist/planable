Socialbee is a pretty capable social media management tool with dozens of features to speed up social media scheduling, planning, and content creation. Still, I tested the platform and I found that its ample feature set comes with a learning curve, which can lead to prolonged setup times and extensive training. Plus, its entry-level and mid-tier pricing plans cap at one workspace, one user, and up to 15 social media accounts (for the mid-tier plan).
